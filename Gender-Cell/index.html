About Us

The Gender Cell at IIT Kanpur is dedicated to creating a safe and inclusive environment for all genders. We strive to raise awareness about gender issues, foster dialogue, and advocate for gender equality in every aspect of campus life.

What We Do

  • Awareness Campaigns: We organize workshops, seminars, and campaigns to raise awareness about gender stereotypes, gender-based violence, and other pertinent issues.

  • Support Services:Our cell provides support and resources for individuals facing gender-based discrimination or harassment. We offer guidance, counseling, and referral services to help ensure the well-being of our community members.

  • Community Engagement: We actively engage with students, faculty, and staff to create a supportive community that values diversity and respects all genders.
